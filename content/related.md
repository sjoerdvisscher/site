2. ["NARCISSUS: Deriving Correct-By-Construction Decoders and Encoders from Binary Formats"](https://www.cs.purdue.edu/homes/bendy/Narcissus/narcissus.pdf) — Suriyakarn, Pit-Claudel, Delaware, Chlipala
